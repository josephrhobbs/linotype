# Continuum Mechanics

~

Our world is filled with constantly moving, constantly changing _continuous media_.  Few things can be reasonably modeled as point masses... and often, the _mechanics of the continuum itself_ is significant for study.  The field of __continuum mechanics__ provides rigorous mathematical tools for the analysis of continuous media.  Three excellent examples of continous media, which are the primary focus of study here, are _linear materials_, _inviscid fluids_, and _Newtonian fluids_.
