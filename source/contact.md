# Contact

~

Thank you for reading _Linotype_.  I'd love to hear from you.  I welcome suggestions for new material, website bug reports, and even guest content!

## Suggestions or Bug Reports

Please contact [editor@linotype.pub](mailto:editor@linotype.pub) with the subject line __SUGGESTION: your suggestion or report__.  I'll be happy to respond to you within the week.

## Collaboration

I'm also open to collaborating on research projects.  Please contant me at [editor@linotype.pub](mailto:editor@linotype.pub) with the subject line __COLLABORATION: title of your project__.

## Guest Content

My goal is to make _Linotype_ a comprehensive, online reference for the intersection between "hardcore" engineering and "elegant" applied mathematics.  I greatly welcome submissions of guest content on _Linotype_ in any technical field related to robotics, mechanics, control theory, optimization, or the theories of probability and stochastic processes.  I'm also open to other technical content that doesn't fit those labels!

If you'd like to submit guest content, please contant [editor@linotype.pub](mailto:editor@linotype.pub) with the subject line __SUBMISSION: title of your submission__.  I prefer to receive submissions typeset using _LaTeX_ as a PDF, with accompanying source _TeX_.  If you have a PDF file with corresponding raw _TeX_, please attach both to your email!  If you have prepared your submission in another way, please send me a raw _TXT_ file of your writing, and pictures of equations and math.  I'll be happy to typeset your equations for publication.

If you're submitting technical reference work or preliminary research that doesn't quite "fit the label", but you think this is the right place to publish it, please tell me why I should include it!

I'll be happy to review your submission within a week.  I'll be sure to explain to you my decision, whether I choose to accept it.  If I choose to include it, you will maintain sole authorship!
