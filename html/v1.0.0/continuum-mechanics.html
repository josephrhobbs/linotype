Our world is filled with constantly moving, constantly changing continuous media. Few things can be reasonably modeled as point masses... and often, the mechanics of the continuum itself is significant for study. The field of continuum mechanics provides rigorous mathematical tools for the analysis of continuous media. Three excellent examples of continous media, which are the primary focus of study here, are linear materials, inviscid fluids, and Newtonian fluids.
