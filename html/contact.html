Thank you for reading Linotype. I'd love to hear from you. I welcome suggestions for new material, website bug reports, and even guest content!

Suggestions or Bug Reports

Please contact editor@linotype.pub with the subject line SUGGESTION: your suggestion or report. I'll be happy to respond to you within the week.

Collaboration

I'm also open to collaborating on research projects. Please contact me at editor@linotype.pub with the subject line COLLABORATION: title of your project.

Guest Content

My goal is to make Linotype a comprehensive, online reference for the intersection between "hardcore" engineering and "elegant" applied mathematics. I greatly welcome submissions of guest content on Linotype in any technical field related to robotics, mechanics, control theory, optimization, or the theories of probability and stochastic processes. I'm also open to other technical content that doesn't fit those labels!

If you'd like to submit guest content, please contact editor@linotype.pub with the subject line SUBMISSION: title of your submission. I prefer to receive submissions typeset using LaTeX as a PDF, with accompanying source TeX. If you have a PDF file with corresponding raw TeX, please attach both to your email! If you have prepared your submission in another way, please send me a raw TXT file of your writing, and pictures of equations and math. I'll be happy to typeset your equations for publication.

If you're submitting technical reference work or preliminary research that doesn't quite "fit the label", but you think this is the right place to publish it, please tell me why I should include it!

I'll be happy to review your submission within a week. I'll be sure to explain to you my decision, whether I choose to accept it. If I choose to include it, you will maintain sole authorship!
